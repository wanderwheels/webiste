Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting a bike or scooter for your Varkala adventure.

What documents do I need to rent a bike in Varkala? expand_more
To rent a scooter or bike with Wander Wheels, you'll need a valid Driving License (two-wheeler class), and a government-issued ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Voter ID). International travelers must present a valid International Driving Permit (IDP) along with their passport. We support completely paperless digital upload for all documents!
What is the price for a scooter rental in Varkala? expand_more
Our affordable scooter rentals start from just ₹600 per day! The exact price depends on the model of the bike (e.g., Honda Activa, Yamaha Ray) and the duration of your trip. We offer significant discounts for weekly and monthly long-term rentals.
Are helmets provided, and is wearing one mandatory? expand_more
Yes, wearing a helmet is strictly mandatory for both the rider and pillion in Kerala. We provide two sanitized, high-quality helmet absolutely free with every rental. Kids helmet is aslo provided upon request.
Can I take the rented two-wheeler outside Varkala? expand_more
Absolutely! You are free to explore nearby coastal towns, backwaters, and beaches. For inter-district or long-distance state travel, please inform us beforehand so we can ensure you have the appropriate touring-ready vehicle and state compliance papers.
What happens if the scooter breaks down? expand_more
We pride ourselves on our meticulously maintained fleet, so breakdowns are extremely rare. However, if you face any mechanical issues, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance across Varkala. We will immediately repair or replace your vehicle so your vacation remains uninterrupted.
Are there any hidden charges like fuel or security deposits? expand_more
We believe in 100% transparent pricing. There are absolutely no hidden charges. Bikes are provided with minimal fuel to reach the nearest petrol pump. A nominal, fully refundable security deposit is collected at the start of your journey which is returned immediately upon safe drop-off of the vehicle.
