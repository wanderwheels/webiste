Bangalore to Varkala: The Ultimate Weekend Escape Guide (2026 Edition)

Bangaloreans, let's be real—sometimes the Cubbon Park greenery just isn't enough. You need the salt air, the red cliffs, and a break from the Silk Board traffic. Varkala is the perfect coastal antidote, and getting there is easier than your daily commute to Whitefield.

By Wander Wheels Team • 5 min read

Phase 1: Getting There (The Bangalore Connection)

The secret to a stress-free Varkala trip? Skip the expensive taxis and the exhausting walks. At Wander Wheels, we make sure your adventure starts the second you step off the train or bus. Most travelers from Namma Bengaluru prefer the overnight journey to wake up fresh by the ocean.

train

By Train (The Comfort Pick)

The most popular choice is the Kanyakumari Express (16526). It's daily, reliable, and drops you right in the heart of town.

  • schedule
    Departure: ~8:00 PM (SBC/KSR Bengaluru or BNC/Bengaluru Cant).
  • location_on
    Arrival: ~11:45 AM (Varkala Sivagiri Station).
  • lightbulb
    Pro-Tip: Book in advance! This train is a favorite for weekenders.

By Bus (The Flexible Pick)

Several premium Volvo and Bharat Benz sleepers run daily from Madiwala, Kalasipalyam, and Hebbal.

  • directions_bus
    Top Operators: Punchiry Travels, Kallada, and SBM Transport.
  • schedule
    Timings: Departures between 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM; Arrival by 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM.
  • pin_drop
    Drop-off: Most buses drop you near Star Cinema or the Varkala Temple Junction.

The "Bangalore to Varkala" 3-Day Itinerary

Make the most of your coastal freedom with our curated weekend guide.

Day 1

Arrival & Cliff Vibes

  • 11:00 AM
    Meet the Wander Wheels team at the station, grab your keys, and head to your resort.
  • 1:00 PM
    Lunch at a North Cliff cafe (try the fresh red snapper or a smoothie bowl).
  • 4:00 PM
    Ride down to South Cliff for a quieter beach experience.
  • Sunset
    Park your bike and walk the promenade. The views are world-class.
Day 2 Morning

The Big Adventures

  • 7:00 AM
    An early start! Ride 35km to Jatayu Earth's Center. The morning breeze on the highway is incredible, and you'll beat the tourist buses.
  • Detour
    If you are an animal lover, stop by Kavery Elephant Park. It's a great spot to experience elephant feeding and rides in a lush setting.
Day 2 Afternoon

Emerald Tunnels & Sunsets

  • 1:00 PM
    Feeling hungry? Stop at Kariyilakallu Toddy Parlour. It's the perfect place to try authentic Kerala toddy paired with spicy local delicacies like fish curry or beef roast.
  • 3:30 PM
    Ride towards the coast and head to Nedungolam for Mangrove Forest Kayaking. It's a peaceful, surreal experience paddling through natural green tunnels.
  • 5:00 PM
    Catch the sunset at Kappil Beach, where the road separates the lake and the sea.
Day 3

Hidden Gems & Departure

Wrap up your perfect weekend trip with a few historic stops before boarding your train or bus back to reality.

  • 9:00 AM
    Ride to Anjengo Fort and the Lighthouse.
  • 12:00 PM
    Quick shopping for spices and incense on the Cliff.
  • 3:00 PM
    Meet us back at the Railway Station/Bus Stand to hand over the keys.
  • Evening
    Board your transport back to Bangalore, arriving just in time for Monday morning.
